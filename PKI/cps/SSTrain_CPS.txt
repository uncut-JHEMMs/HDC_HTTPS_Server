This document sets forthe policies related to the issuance and management of certificates.

The SSTrain AS exists solely for the purpose of training and education, and shouldn't be
trusted anywhere outside of an educational setting. Keys and certificates are issued on
an as-needed basis for the purpose of fulfilling educational goals, and can be revoked 
for any or no reason without warning. Keys and certificates are stored on the user's 
personal computer encrypted with a password, but may be moved to other machines as 
needed to fulfill educational goals.
